COMMUNITY GUIDELINES

Last updated: June 12, 2026

Nomeo is a place for writers and readers to share ideas, learn, and connect. These Community Guidelines explain what is and isn't allowed. They apply to everyone — readers, creators, and visitors — and to all content and interactions on the Platform, including posts, comments, lounges, messages, profiles, and topic suggestions.

Breaking these Guidelines can lead to content removal, warnings, restrictions, suspension, or a permanent ban. Serious violations may be reported to authorities.


WHAT WE EXPECT

Be respectful. Disagree with ideas, not with people. No personal attacks.

Be honest. Don't impersonate others or misrepresent who you are.

Be original. Only publish work you own or have the right to share. Credit sources.

Respect privacy. Don't share other people's private information without consent.


WHAT IS NOT ALLOWED

Harassment and hate. Bullying, threats, stalking, or targeted harassment. Hate speech or content that attacks or demeans people based on race, ethnicity, religion, gender, sexual orientation, disability, or similar characteristics. Encouraging others to harass or harm anyone.

Violence and danger. Threats of violence or content that incites or glorifies it. Content promoting terrorism, violent extremism, or organised crime. Instructions for creating weapons or other tools intended to cause serious harm.

Child safety. Any content that sexualises, endangers, or exploits minors is strictly prohibited and will be removed and reported. This is a zero-tolerance area.

Sexual content. Sexually explicit content is prohibited on the Platform, including non-consensual intimate imagery.

Illegal and harmful activity. Promoting or facilitating illegal activity. Selling regulated or prohibited goods. Fraud, scams, or deceptive schemes.

Spam and manipulation. Spam, repetitive posting, or misuse of comments, follows, and messages. Artificially inflating reads, likes, follows, or earnings. Malware, phishing, or attempts to compromise the Platform or its users.

Misinformation. Content that causes real-world harm through false claims, for example dangerous health misinformation.

Intellectual property. Posting content that infringes copyrights, trademarks, or other rights. Plagiarism or passing off others' work as your own.


CREATORS AND EARNINGS

Creators must not manipulate the earnings system. Using bots, fake accounts, paid engagement, or any scheme to inflate reading time or earnings is prohibited and may result in forfeited earnings and a ban.


LOUNGES

Lounge hosts are responsible for keeping their spaces within these Guidelines. Hosts may set additional reasonable rules for their lounge, but cannot override these Guidelines.


REPORTING

If you see content or behaviour that breaks these Guidelines, use the report feature or contact support@nomeo.com. Reports are reviewed by our moderation team. Do not abuse the reporting system with false reports.


ENFORCEMENT

We may, at our discretion and without prior notice, remove, hide, or restrict content; issue warnings; limit account features or visibility; suspend or permanently ban accounts; and withhold or reverse creator earnings in cases of abuse.

We aim to apply these Guidelines fairly and consistently, but we may take stronger action for severe or repeated violations.


APPEALS

If you believe an enforcement action was a mistake, you may appeal by contacting support@nomeo.com. Provide your account details and an explanation. We will review and respond.


CHANGES

We may update these Guidelines as the Platform evolves. Continued use after changes take effect means you accept them.

Questions? Contact support@nomeo.com.
